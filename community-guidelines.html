Community Guidelines

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Protect Sensitive Data

Use AiScrubber to reduce exposure, but do not share data you are not authorized to process. Review every export before using it elsewhere.

Be Respectful

Feedback, issue reports, and collaboration requests should be constructive, specific, and respectful.

No Abuse

Do not use Cypher tools or community spaces for harassment, malware, credential theft, doxxing, spam, or unlawful activity.

Responsible Security Reports

If you find a vulnerability, report it privately through the contact channels and include enough detail to reproduce the issue safely.
