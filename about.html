Our Genesis

AiScrubber was born from a stark realization: Generative AI is accelerating faster than corporate compliance can govern it. Professionals worldwide are weaponizing LLMs for productivity, often blind to the fact that they are silently leaking highly sensitive intellectual property into public training sets.

The Architecture of Trust

We built this tool under one absolute paradigm: Zero-Trust. We do not have servers. We do not have databases recording your keystrokes. Text is sanitized locally in your browser, and recovery data stays in your own local storage until you reset it.
