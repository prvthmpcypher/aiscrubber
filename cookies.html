Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Cookies

AiScrubber does not intentionally set tracking cookies. The static site is designed without account cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies.

Local Storage

The workspace uses browser localStorage for recovery, export flow state, and optional feedback. localStorage is not a cookie, but it persists in your browser until reset or cleared.

Third Parties

External CDNs may be requested by your browser to load fonts and JavaScript libraries. Those providers may process network metadata according to their own policies.

Clearing Data

You can clear AiScrubber local data by using the workspace reset flow or clearing this site's browser storage.
